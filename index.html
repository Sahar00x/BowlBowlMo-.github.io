HOME

Ramen has been known for being an infamous Japanese delicacy. They say you will only taste the originality of it from Japan itself, which is exactly why we have established Bowl Bowl Mo. Bowl Bowl Mo allows us to enjoy this sophisticated dish from home with just a simple 5-10 minute boiling process. Our storefront where we sell our cooked and ready made ramen is located at Calamba, Laguna. If you won't be able to access our storefront's location, fear not, for we sell both our cookable ramen packs and our actual dishes on Grab food and Foodpanda.

PRODUCTS

Ramen Packs

Our JaPANIS ramen is a parody of traditional Japanese ramen. It consists of noodles made from a dough of wheat, flour, water, and salt, 2 halves of a soy sauce simmered boiled egg. The noodles also come with other toppings like narutomaki fishcake made of ounces non-oily white fish filets, such as Alaska pollock or southern blue whiting, an egg white, a teaspoon of salt, a teaspoon of sugar, a teaspoon of mirin, and red food coloring, as well as chopped up spring onions, sliced beef, wakame seaweed, and sweet corn. (Toppings come in a seperate packet.)

Our CalaMAASIM ramen is a parody of Laksa ramen originating from southeast asia. It consists of noodles made from a dough of wheat, flour, water, and salt. The noodles will already have flavoring so you only have to add boiling water and the broth will still taste like original Laksa broth. Toppings include 2 halves of calamansi, a Thai bird chili, 3 small chicken wedges, along with chopped green onions. (Toppings come in a seperate packet.)

Our KimchiU ramen is a parody of Kimchi ramen originating from Korea. It consists of noodles made from a dough of wheat, flour, water, and salt. The noodles will already have flavoring so you only have to add boiling water and the broth will still taste like original Kimchi soup broth. It's toppings include Kimchi, 2 halves of a soy sauce simmered boiled egg, ginger, 2 thin slices of Shiitake mushrooms, along with chopped green onions. (Toppings come in a seperate packet.)

Our PorkNAT ramen is a parody of Tonkotsu ramen originating from Japan. It consists of noodles made from a dough of wheat, flour, water, and salt. The noodles will already have flavoring so you only have to add boiling water and the broth will still taste like original Tonkotsu soup broth. It's toppings include Pork and pork bones for the broth, 1 half of a soy sauce simmered boiled egg, small slices of narutomaki fishcake, along with chopped green onions. (Toppings come in a seperate packet.)
